MindFrame

MindFrame is a research platform for delivering structured, evidence-based psychological interactions via conversational AI. It enables supervised therapeutic and research conversations, detailed data collection, and real-time summaries of performance and data collected.

About mindframe

MindFrame supports transparent, auditable conversational AI for psychological interventions, using structured templates defined in plain text. Every decision made by the system can be traced, reproduced, and adapted.
